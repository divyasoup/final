Passing Time

Passing Time

2023. Oil on Canvas. 16 inches by 20 inches

Passing Time is the first part of a three part art sequence. Using three canvases of the same size, Divya portrayed herself sitting on the bus in several different atmospheres. These are meant to show the passage of the days and of time, as well as the lonely nature of travel.
